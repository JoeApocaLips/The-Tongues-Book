- A fragment or short sentence, lowercase. Punctuation sparse and expressive — a dash, a comma, an ellipsis — never a full stop.
- Clinical or disturbing. Never decorative. Never poetic for its own sake.

- disposable camera / polaroid / instax → intimate, private, domestic, slightly damaged
- webcam / CCTV / VHS → surveillance, data, record — but vary: could be medical, industrial, domestic
